Radar Notes: The Quiet Moments Before Launch

A quick look at how JetSignal picks out rare aircraft and why spotters love a clean signal.

JetSignal is built for those calm, electric moments when a rare tail suddenly appears on the scope. We’re using AI to detect patterns from public feeds and highlight the traffic you actually care about.

Signal over noise

Our alert engine looks for unusual aircraft types, special liveries, and route deviations in real time. You’ll see fewer, sharper pings and more time outside with your camera ready.

Local-first alerts

Every alert is tuned to your home airport. We surface only what’s likely to be visible in your airspace, with precise ETAs and heads-up timing.

What’s next

Our beta will open with advanced filters, aircraft watchlists, and spotter stats. You’ll be able to track your personal highlights and compare routes across the community.
